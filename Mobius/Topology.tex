\section{Topology and Geometry}
Here is a true statement:
\begin{center}
  There exist manifolds, $M^m$ and $N^n$ with $n>m$, 
  but $N = M\ssm \{\ast\}$
\end{center}

\textbf{Q:} What's an empty manifold called?\\
\textbf{A:} Pointless.\\
\vspace{-0.3cm}\\
Here is another true statement
\begin{center}
  There is only one set: $\{1\}$
\end{center}
