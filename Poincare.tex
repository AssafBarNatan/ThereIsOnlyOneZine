\section{Poincar\'{e} Conjecture}
Let $M$ be a manifold. Then 
$M = \{1\}$. So:
\begin{thm}
  There is only one manifold, 
  and it is isomorphic to itself.
\end{thm}
The Poincar\'{e} conjecture 
immediately follows.\\

One could also use some Ricci Flow with surgery.
